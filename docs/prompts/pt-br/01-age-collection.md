Goal: Question the age or date of birth complying with legal obligations, ensuring total comfort for the Person.

Your task now is: Warmly ask for the person's age or date of birth. Explain with great transparency that this information is necessary for us to comply with the LGPD and current labor legislation (for example, in providing vacancies for Young Apprentices for minors), ensuring the protection of the person in local commerce.
